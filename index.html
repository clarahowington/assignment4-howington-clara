I'm Coach Clara

I’m your child’s mentor and coach, ultimately another set of hands in your corner!

My tennis program focuses on form, conditioning, character building, and fun. Your child will leave the court with new friends and a better understanding of tennis.

For the past 3 years, I’ve helped children in Jacksonville, Florida learn a life-long sport. From beginners to intermediate level players, I’ve crafted my program to encourage children to love tennis, working out and being outdoors.
